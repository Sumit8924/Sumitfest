About GIFT Autonomous College

GIFT AUTONOMOUS

Igniting Minds, Empowering Futures

Gandhi Institute of Fashion and Textile (GIFT) Autonomous College, Bhubaneswar is a premier institution established in 1997. The college has been consistently ranked among the top technical institutions in Odisha and Eastern India.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and industry-oriented curriculum, GIFT has been producing technically skilled professionals who are making significant contributions to various industries.
